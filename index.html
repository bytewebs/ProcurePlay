Rules:

  • You start with a bank balance of $1000.
  • You have 10 seconds to fulfill each order.
  • Ordering from China costs $5 per unit.
  • Ordering from Mexico costs $8 per unit.
  • Your goal is to maximize profit by managing inventory efficiently.

How to Play:

  1. Monitor the demand displayed on the screen.
  2. Decide whether to order from China or Mexico based on cost and demand.
  3. Enter the quantity you wish to order and click the corresponding order button.
  4. Watch your bank balance and stock level to make informed decisions.
  5. Try to balance inventory costs with potential profits to succeed!
